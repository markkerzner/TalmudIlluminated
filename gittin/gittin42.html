Gittin 42 - An Ox Gores a Half-Slave

If an ox gores a half-slave, then on master's day the damages go to the master, otherwise, to the slave. - Then can he marry a slavewoman one day and a free women the other? - No, laws of marriage can't be learned from the laws of money.





If a master intentionally knocks out a slave's tooth or eye or any other of the 22 protrusions, the slave goes out to freedom, but needs a Get.
